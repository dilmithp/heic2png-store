About HEIC2PNG.Store

Your trusted partner for free, secure, and lightning-fast HEIC to PNG image conversion

Our Mission

At HEIC2PNG.Store, our mission is simple yet powerful: to provide the most secure, efficient, and user-friendly image conversion tools that respect your privacy while delivering exceptional results. We believe that converting your images should be free, fast, and completely secure.

We understand the frustration of dealing with incompatible image formats, especially when Apple's HEIC format isn't supported on all platforms. Our goal is to bridge this compatibility gap seamlessly.

Why We Built This Tool

With the widespread adoption of HEIC format by Apple devices starting with iOS 11, millions of users face compatibility issues when sharing photos across different platforms. Traditional online converters often compromise your privacy by uploading files to servers.

We created HEIC2PNG.Store to solve these problems by providing a completely client-side conversion tool that processes your images locally in your browser, ensuring your privacy is never compromised.

What Makes Us Different

Privacy First Approach

Unlike other online converters, we process everything locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. No uploads, no data collection, no tracking of your images.

Advanced Technology

We use cutting-edge web technologies and optimized algorithms to ensure the fastest possible conversion while maintaining perfect image quality. Our tool works entirely in your browser using modern JavaScript APIs.

Universal Accessibility

Our converter works on all modern devices and browsers - Windows, Mac, iOS, Android, Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. No software installation required, just open and convert.

Completely Free Forever

We believe image conversion should be free for everyone. No hidden fees, no registration requirements, no file limits, and no watermarks. Our service is supported by non-intrusive advertisements.

Our Technology Stack

Client-Side Processing

Our converter uses advanced JavaScript libraries and Web APIs to process HEIC files directly in your browser. This means faster conversion, better privacy, and no server dependencies. The entire conversion happens on your device.

  • Modern ES6+ JavaScript
  • WebAssembly for performance
  • Canvas API for image processing
  • File API for secure file handling

Progressive Web App

HEIC2PNG.Store is built as a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means it works offline, loads instantly, and provides a native app-like experience on all devices. You can even add it to your home screen for quick access.

  • Service Worker for offline functionality
  • Responsive design for all screen sizes
  • Fast loading with optimized assets
  • Cross-platform compatibility

Quality Preservation

We use lossless conversion algorithms that maintain the original image quality, resolution, and color depth. Your converted PNG files will have the same visual quality as the original HEIC files, just with universal compatibility.

  • Lossless PNG compression
  • Original resolution preservation
  • Color profile maintenance
  • Metadata handling options
