HEIC2PNG.Store is committed to protecting your privacy. Our HEIC to PNG converter operates entirely in your browser, which means your images never leave your device and we never see or store your files.

How Our Service Works

HEIC2PNG.Store provides a client-side image conversion service that processes your files locally in your web browser. This approach ensures maximum privacy and security:

  • No File Uploads: Your images are processed entirely in your browser
  • No Server Storage: We never store, access, or transmit your images
  • Complete Privacy: Your files remain on your device at all times
  • Instant Processing: No waiting for uploads or downloads

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do NOT collect any personal information such as:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Account credentials or login information
  • Payment information (our service is free)
  • Personal photos or image content

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited, non-personal technical information to improve our service:

  • IP address and general geographic location (country/city level)
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Device information and screen resolution
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring website information
  • Anonymous usage statistics and performance metrics

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted

Our website is hosted and operated from the United States. If you access our service from outside the US, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Data Retention

Since we don't collect or store your images or personal information, there is no data retention period for your files. Any technical data we collect is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, typically no longer than 26 months.
