Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is your converter?

Our converter is 100% secure! All conversions happen locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. No uploads, no data collection, no tracking.

Is there a file size limit?

We recommend files under 50MB for optimal performance, but there's no strict limit. Larger files may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities and available memory.

Do you support batch conversion?

Yes! You can convert multiple HEIC files to PNG simultaneously. Simply select multiple files or drag and drop them all at once. Our tool will process them in batches for efficiency.

Is the service really free?

Absolutely! Our converter is completely free with no hidden charges, registration requirements, or usage limitations. We support the service through non-intrusive advertisements.

What browsers are supported?

Our converter works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. It's compatible with Windows, Mac, iOS, and Android devices.

Can I use this offline?

Once the page is loaded, the converter can work offline since all processing happens in your browser. However, you'll need an internet connection to initially load the page and libraries.
